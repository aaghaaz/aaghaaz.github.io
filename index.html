About Us

Aaghaaz is the annual cultural fest of School of Engineering and Applied Science (SEAS). It provides a unique opportunity to all the students to indulge into various extracurricular activities and help them escape their souls from all the worry and anxiety of the world they live in.


This cultural extravaganza helps them unmask the masked flair in them by challenging their intellect, creativity and mindfulness through a range of activities and enhance their experience and make them a remarkable one. This unique confluence of art, culture, dance and drama will make you crave for more. So come, revive, reverb and rejoice with us.
